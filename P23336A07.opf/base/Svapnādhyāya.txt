namo buddhāya||svapnādhyāyaṃ pravakṣyāmi bṛhaspativinirmitaṃ|yena vijñāyate puṃsāṃ svapneṣu
ca śubhāśubhaṃ

svapnas tu prathame <yāme> samvatsaraphalapradaḥ|dvitīye tv aṣṭabhir māsai(r māsais tri)bhis
tṛtīyake

caturbhis tu caturthe syāt phalaṃ suniyataṃ nṛṇāṃ|aruṇodayavelāyāṃ daśāhena phalaṃ bhavet

śastraprahāra<ḥ> kṛmipūrṇadeho raṇacyuto gātraviśo(dhanaṃ) ca|viṣṭhānulepo rudhirasrutiṣ ca
svapneṣvagamyāgamanaṃ ca dhānyaṃ

ārohaṇaṃ govṛṣakuñjarāṇāṃ prāsādaśailāgravanaspatīnāṃ|āruhya nāvaṃ prati(gṛhya) vīṇāṃ bhuktvā
ruditvā dhruvam arthalābhaḥ

yas tu paśyati svapnānte rājānaṃ kuñjaraṃ hayaṃ|suvarṇaṃ govṛṣaṃ gāṃ ca kuṭumbaṃ tasyavardhate

kṣīriṇaṃ phalinaṃ vṛkṣa(m e)kākī yas tu rohati|tatrasthaś ca vibhudhyeta dhanaṃ śīghramavāpnuyāt

prāsādasthas tu yo bhuktvā samudrānte pibejjalaṃ|api dāsakule jātaḥ so'pi rājā bhavi(ṣyati)

dīpam anna<ṃ> phala<ṃ> kanyā<ṃ> chattraṃ rathaṃ dhvajaṃ|yas tu paśyati svapnānte tāny eva labhatedhruvaṃ

mānuṣāṇi <ca> māṃsāni svapnānte yas tu bhakṣayet|pāde pañcaśataṃ (lābhaḥ) sahasraṃ bāhubhakṣaṇe

śiraso bhakṣaṇe rājyaṃ labhate puṇyavigrahaḥ|bahukoṭīśvaro cāpi kadā cij jāyate pumān

astrair veṣṭayate yas tu gṛhaṃ vā nagarāṇi ca|gṛhe maṇḍalarājyaṃ vā nagare pārthivobhavet

upānahau ca cchattraṃ ca labdhvā yaḥpratibudhyati|asiṃ ca nirmalaṃ tīkṣṇaṃ tasya śrīssarvatomukhī

naukārūḍho vipannaḥ syāt tato'pi ca samuttaret|pravāsaṃ nirdiśet tasya śīghraṃ ca punar āgamaṃ

kharoṣṭravasayo jānaiḥ svapnānte yas turohayet|tatrastho'py avabudhyeta aiśvaryamadhigacchati

dantā yasya viśīryante uttiṣṭhanti patanti ca|bandhunāśo bhavet tasya apatyaṃ cavinaśyati

abhyaṅgatas tu tailena medasā<ca> ghṛtena ca|vyādhiṃ tasya vinirdiśet

raktāmbaradharo bhūto nīyate dhakṣināṃ diśaṃ|kṛṣṇanāryā kare gṛhya mṛtyu<ṃ> tasya vinirdiśet

raktāmbaradharāṃ nārīṃ raktagandhānulepitāṃ|avagūhati yaḥ svapne tasya rātrir a<pa>ścimā

śuklāmbaradharāṃ nārīṃ śuklagandhānulepitāṃ|avagūhati yaḥ svapne tasya śrī<ḥ> sarvatomukhī

rātrau suptas tu yaḥ kaś cij jalokābhis tudaśyate|kanyā tu jāyate tasya nātra kāryavicāraṇā

abhidravanti yaṃ svapne śṛṅgiṇo daṃṣṭriṇo'pi vāvānaro vā<va>rāho <vā> bhaved rājakulād bhayaṃ

ādityamaṇḍalaṃ svapne candraṃ vā yadi paśyati|sa puṃsāṃ labhate koṣaṃ gṛhaṃ vā saṃprayacchati

nigaḍair badhyate yas tu bāhupāśena vā punaḥ|putro vā jāyate tasya pratiṣṭhā vāpinirdiśyet

yas taptānatva sarpe'pi daṃkṣyate dakṣiṇebhuje|sahasralābho bhavet tasya sampūrṇe daśamedine

raktaṃ pibati yaḥ svapne surām vāpi kathañcana|brāḥmaṇo labhate vidhyām itaro labhate dhanaṃ

kṣīraṃ pi<bati> yaḥ svapne saphenaṃ dohinīgataṃ|somapānaṃ bhavet tasya anyāṃ vā bhūtimādiśet

dadhi dattvā bhavet prītiḥ godhūmaiś cadhanāgamaḥ|yavair yajñāgamaṃ vidyāt lābhaḥ siddhārthakairapi

puṣpite puṣpitaṃ kāryaṃ phalite vṛddhimuttamām|dhūmāyamāne dhūmāyat jvalate cārtham āvehet

āsane śayane vāpi śārīre vāhane gṛhe|jvalamāne vibudhyeta tasya śrīḥsarvatomukhī

sa vyādhimukto nirujo
